About

Chris Jackiewicz, the Founder of FinAid Master, is an education professional with over a decade of experience working with students and families to help them proactively navigate the nuances of financing higher education. Chris earned his MBA in Finance from Drexel University, graduating at the top of his class with a 4.0 GPA. While working in the Financial Aid Office of a major research institution in Philadelphia, he has seen the stress students experience when they do not proactively think through how to finance their education before enrolling. The right counsel at the right time could have prevented major hardship and this experience called Chris to action to be an advocate to students and their families.
